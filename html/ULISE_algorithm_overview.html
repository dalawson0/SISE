ULISE

Unified Linear Input & State Estimator for linear time-invariant systems (zero-step state, one-step input delay)

Syntax:

[xhat_UL , Px_UL, dhat_UL, Pd_UL, Pxd_UL] = ULISE(A, B, C, D, G, H, Q, R, K, u, y, xhat0, P_x0)

Description:

Assumptions:

  • System is linear, time-invariant, and discrete-time.
  • Initial state estimate and covariance are provided.
  • Process and measurement noises are zero-mean, white Gaussian noise.
  • The process and measurement noise covariances must be positive (semi-)definite matrices i.e., and .
